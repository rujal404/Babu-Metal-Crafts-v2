How can we help you ?

Step into the serene world of Babu Metal Crafts, a cherished heritage handicraft studio in the heart of Patan. Just moments away from revered world heritage sites, our workshop is a cultural haven for art lovers from around the globe. Adorned with Patan’s timeless creations from traditional metal statues to intricate handcrafted masterpieces. Babu Metal Crafts is your gateway to authentic Nepalese artistry.
